About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I work on aesthetics, ethics, philosophy of religion, and the interplay between these fields.

Broadly, I ask how we can shift our understanding of aesthetic experience in a way that allows us to have a more expansive, richer, and more morally meaningful engagement with beauty.

I am currently seeking to do this by engaging Kant and his romantic successors on the roles of nature, genius, and symbolic reflection, as well as the contemporary critiques of such notions.
